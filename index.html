WELCOME TO STUDENT REGISTRATION

This assignment is a web-based application that allows students to fill in their information also capture their digital signature using a canvas and then automatically generate a stylish info card. The card can be previewed in real-time and finally downloaded as an image (PNG). It provides a simple way to create digital visiting cards / mini ID cards without needing external software.
